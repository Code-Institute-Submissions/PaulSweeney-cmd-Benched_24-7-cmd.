Sportive Wear Collaboration


The rise in sportswear sales due to COVID-19 isolation means that there is more room than ever for new competitors, so here at Benched 24/7 we have collaborated with SportiveWear to give you the opportunity to not only focus on your health and well being but actually make a career out of it! SportiveWear are always on the look out for potential new ambassadors to represent their image and they have exclusive rights with us as a joint partnership to discover the next faces for their exiting brand which has featured in various online sportswear retailers and supplement sites such as "GrowTense", "Compound Structure" and "GFlex - 5". The chosen winners will go on to have an exclusive contract with SW with the potential for some serious exposure within the sports industry. We will update the site regularly! Registration cannot be done online so come and see us at the reception desk for further details and requirements.

Please note you MUST be a member at this gym for a minimum of 3 months to register.
