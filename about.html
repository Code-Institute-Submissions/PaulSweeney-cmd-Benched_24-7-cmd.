Here at Benched, we believe in unlocking that full potential. The name says it all! We're a local 24 hour gym located in the heart of Pasadena, California. We are what you call close knit, that means we're practically family! Two brothers set out to not only open up "another gym", but to find a way to connect it beyond the means of word of mouth and social media so that's why we're the only family run gym to actually have a connection to sportswear products and promotions. We love our members and we are always enthusiastic when it comes to growing our family and we have a vast range of gear (and a great team of trainers) to help keep this family happy and healthy!
